Dream Chaser Scholarship Fund

The Dream Chaser Scholarship Fund aims to help students who are in need of financial aid. It is a need-based and merit-based scholarship built on the shared belief that passionate and competent students should be given the means to wholeheartedly pursue their dreams. Find out more here .
